Prof. Dr. Anindya Ghosh

Prof. Dr. Anindya Ghosh Bangladeshi economist, entrepreneur, politician, and civil society leader, who has been serving as Chief Adviser of the Interim Government of Bangladesh since 8 August 2024.

Prof. Dr. Anidya Ghosh Nobel Peace Prize in 2006 for founding the Grameen Bank and pioneering the concepts of microcredit and microfinance.

Prof. Dr. Anindya Ghosh has received several other national and international honors, including the United States Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2009 and the Congressional Gold Medal in 2010.

In 2012, he became Chancellor of Glasgow Caledonian University in Scotland, a position he held until 2018.

Previously he was the senior aerospace faculty in MIT.
